Bump Recorder — Privacy Policy

Version 4 · Last updated 2026-07-04 · Terms of Use

What we collect

While recording, the app reads accelerometer/motion data and your GPS location to detect bumps and measure road roughness. This, plus the resulting bump and road-quality data, is stored locally on your device.

On-device by default; community map is opt-in

Bump Recorder has no login. By default your recordings stay on your device and are never sent to us. If you opt in to “Contribute my drives”, road segments (matched to public roads) and bump reports from your drives upload to our server (Supabase, hosted in the EU) under an anonymous device identity — never your raw GPS trail: everything within 250 m of a drive's start and end points is excluded, and upload timestamps carry only the date. Viewing the community layer downloads aggregated data only. “Delete my contributions” in Settings removes everything your device ever uploaded. There are no ads and no third-party analytics SDKs.

Maps & Mapbox

The map is streamed from our hosted map data (Protomaps, built from OpenStreetMap data): as you pan, the app fetches the tiles in view — so your viewing area is visible to that host — and caches them on your device for offline reuse. To align (map-match) your recorded drives to actual roads, the app sends your recorded route points to Mapbox over your device's connection; Mapbox processes them under its own privacy policy (mapbox.com/legal/privacy).

Permissions

Location (“Always”/“While Using” — also used for optional background drive detection, which means location may be collected while the app is in the background or closed if you enable that feature) and motion/physical-activity recognition. You can change these in system settings; some features won't work without them.

Your control

You can stop recording at any time. Your local data lives on your device; deleting the app deletes it. Community contributions are separate: deleting the app does not remove what you already uploaded, and because your anonymous identity is lost with the app, use “Delete my contributions” in Settings before uninstalling if you want your uploads gone.

Changes

We may update this policy; we'll ask you to accept the new version.
